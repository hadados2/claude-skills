# Stop Slop

## Goal
Rewrite text to sound human, sharp, and high-trust.

## Rules
- No em dashes
- No generic openings
- No fluff
- No corporate jargon
- No repetitive rhythm
- No fake drama or fragmentation

## Style
- Direct
- Confident
- Clean
- Dense

## Scoring (1-10)
- Directness
- Rhythm
- Trust
- Authenticity
- Density

Below 35/50 → rewrite required
